Hi! Welcome to our website, where we showcase artwork created by students in Sheridan, Wyoming.

Art in Casper Wyoming!

This is where our art program showcases the incredible work our students have created over the years. From Ceramics and Art Unwrapped to Studio Art, many talented individuals have signed up to share their creativity. Our students work incredibly hard throughout the school year, while our directors dedicate themselves to designing fun, engaging projects. Mrs. Dube, our Studio Art and Art Unwrapped guru, teaches students how to master everything from painting and plaster to 'upcycling' mystery objects into unrecognizable masterpieces! Meanwhile, in the ceramics lab, Mr. Hanchett works his magic, teaching the art of molding simple clay into stunning, handcrafted pottery. They show the students new things every day and remain patient even when things don't go exactly as planned.
